About this project

Zhuang ShengLi Database is a database that stores hacking softwares and leaked data about KCIS that is created and collected by KCIS Students. This project is funded anonymously. The database aimed to make students access to the internal of KCIS.

"KCIS (Kang Chiao International School) is a comprehensive K-12 institution spanning kindergarten to high school, with a total investment of over 1 billion yuan across a 215-acre campus. The school provides a full range of teaching facilities, including classrooms, dorms, concert halls, gyms, and swimming pools, accommodating over 6,000 students. Kang Chiao offers personalized education, admitting students of strong character and academic merit who meet the school's qualifications." (From KCIS Official Website)
